Sadiye

This tomb is located at the end of Bustan Street and next to Delgosha Garden at the foot of the mountain in the northeast of Shiraz. Around the tomb, there are many graves of religious leaders who are buried there according to their will. One of the most important of them is Shorideh Shirazi, whose tomb is connected to Saadi's tomb by a porch.
